About Prescription Tracker

Never forget to refill your prescription anymore!

The Prescription Tracker will track all your prescriptions and send you reminders for taking medicine, keeping track of your pills left till next refill, how many refills you have left, reminder to contact your doctor if you have 0 refills and need doctors authorization etc.

A Dashboard will show each user impending reminders and the status of recent reminders as taken, missed, skipped or snoozed. Feedback can be garnered via reports which will display the user's track record with taking their meds. A simple report which lists the user's medications can also be generated.Â  These reports can be sent to the user via email.
